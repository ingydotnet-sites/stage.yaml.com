    Contact Us About YES¶

    Interested in joining the YES Program? We'd love to hear from you.

    Get in Touch¶

    • YES Program Inquiries

      yes@yaml.com

      Questions about joining, pricing, or benefits

    • Security Issues

      security@yaml.com

      Report vulnerabilities in YAML projects

    • General Questions

      info@yaml.com

      Everything else - media, partnerships, general inquiries

    What Happens Next?¶

    1. Initial Conversation - We'll discuss your organization's YAML usage and needs
    2. Tier Recommendation - We'll help you choose the right tier and benefits
    3. Proposal - You'll receive a formal proposal with membership agreement
    4. Onboarding - Once signed, we'll get you set up with all your benefits
    5. Benefit Selection - Choose your additional benefits from your tier's menu
    6. Welcome Package - Receive your badges, forum access, and welcome materials

    Information for Your Request¶

    When reaching out, it helps to include:

    • Organization name and brief description
    • Your role and decision-making process
    • YAML usage - Which YAML libraries/tools you depend on
    • Primary interests - What benefits matter most to you
    • Timeline - When you're looking to join
    • Questions - Anything you'd like to discuss
